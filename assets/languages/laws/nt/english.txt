<h1>Primary Act:</h1>

<h2>Anti Discrimination Act 1992</h2>
<h2>Section/s Applicable:</h2>
<h2>Section 21, Discrimination by Refusal to Allow Guide Dog. </h2>
<h2>Excerpt from Section 21:</h2>
<p>“ (1) For the purposes of this Act, a person discriminates on the ground of impairment against a person with a visual, hearing or mobility impairment if the person treats the person with the impairment less favourably because the person has a guide dog. “</p>


<h2>Maximum Fine for Breach of Act:</h2>
<p>Fixed penalty of $500</p>
