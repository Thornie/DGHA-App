<p>Guide Dog Handlers and their Guide Dogs are legally allowed to enter public places including:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Shops and supermarkets</li>
    <li>Cafes and restaurants</li>
    <li>Pubs and clubs</li>
    <li>Cinemas and theatres</li>
    <li>Hotels, motels and other accommodation</li>
    <li>Medical/dental practices and hospitals (except in an operating theatre)</li>
    <li>All forms of public transport, including taxis, buses, trains, trams and airplanes</li>
</ul>
<p>Guide Dog access rights are governed by the Disability Discrimination Act 1992 (Section 9), National Disability Services Standards (2014), Privacy Act 1986 (Commonwealth), and Dog Act 1976.</p>
<p>For more information visit <a href="http://guidedogsaustralia.com/">Guide Dogs Australia</a></p>
