<h1>Primary Act:</h1>

<h2>Dog and Cat Management Act 1995</h2>
<h2>Section/s Applicable:</h2>
<h2>Section 21, Accreditation of Disability Dogs, Guide Dogs etc</h2>
<h2>Section 81, Disability Dogs, Guide Dogs etc</h2>
<h2>Excerpt from Section 81:</h2>

<p> <ul>“(1) Despite this Act or any other Act—
        <li>(a) a person who is wholly or partially blind or deaf, or otherwise disabled, is entitled to be accompanied by an accredited disability dog, guide dog or hearing dog in a public place or public passenger vehicle; and</li>
        <li>(b) the occupier or person in charge of a public place or public passenger vehicle must not refuse access to the place or vehicle to a person who is wholly or partially blind or deaf, or otherwise disabled, on the ground that the person is accompanied by a disability dog, guide dog or hearing dog.”</li>
    </ul>

</p>

<h2>Maximum Fine for Breach of Act:</h2>
<p> $250</p>
<h2>Exceptions to Access Under Act:</h2>
<p>• It is not unlawful for a person with an assistance animal to be asked to produce evidence that their dog is an assistance animal.</p>
